## Wall-clock model update

The Phase 5 dropping of the `packages.ncps` monolith now reveals an
even cleaner end state. Max-of-cohorts (the parallel Nix wall-clock
once the monolith is gone):

    Phase 3 (with schema-equivalence-check): max(6m01s, 3m52s, 2m19s,
        2m12s, 1m12s, 1m08s, ...) ≈ 6m
    Phase 4 (without schema-equivalence-check): max(5m16s, 3m20s,
        1m51s, 1m51s, 1m50s, 1m07s, ...) ≈ 5m

Phase 4 alone shaves ~1m off the projected post-monolith wall-clock,
on top of removing 1m12s of duplicate work from the sequential sum.
